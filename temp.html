Summary: Latitude vs. Temperature

As you approach the equator from both the northern and southern hemispheres, temperature increases.

In March, the northern hemisphere is tilted away from the sun.
Therefore, the northern hemisphere has a more dramatic increase in temperature as it approaches the equator.

The southern hemisphere is tilted toward the sun.
Therefore, the southern hemisphere has a steady increase from 40 degrees to 100 degrees.
